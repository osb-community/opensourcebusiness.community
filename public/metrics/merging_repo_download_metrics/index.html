Open Source Business Metrics: Correated Repo & Download

Metric Description:

Another powerful indicator of potential customers is the number of those who download your product and then open issues in GitHub or commit code to your repositories.

Example Metrics:

  • # of people or orgs that have downloaded and then down something in your issue tracker or repo

Goals:

Engaged users are less likely to churn or explore other objects. If a user not only uses your software on the regaular basis but is asking questions and contributing the more likely they will stay users.
